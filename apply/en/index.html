Call to Action

What is it about?

Our project is aimed at all those who love foreign languages and the world of entertainment. If you are passionate about YouTube videos, dubbing and translation, this is the perfect project for you! You will work closely with a young and dynamic team that will provide you with all the necessary resources to translate and dub the videos of our influencers.

If you are ready to join our team, do not hesitate to apply! We are always looking for new talents ready to take on new challenges.

What to expect

100% Remote

We are a team of professionals working remotely from all over the world, offering our collaborators the opportunity to work from anywhere and at any time. You can manage your time and activities independently without sacrificing work quality.

Work as much as you want

We offer the opportunity to work as much as you want, based on your needs and availability. You can manage your hours and workload independently without sacrificing work quality. Whether you can dedicate just a few hours a week or every day, this project is perfect for you.

Above-average compensation

If you are ready to work with consistency and dedication and you can stand out for your qualities, after a few months, you could earn several thousand euros. The more you commit, the higher your compensation will be.

What You Will Do

As a VidLingo collaborator, you will be responsible for translating and dubbing videos from one or more YouTube channels that we will assign to you.
You will have the opportunity to work with great flexibility, according to your needs and availability.
For each assigned channel, your earnings will be equal to 50% of the profits generated by the channel itself, and they may increase based on your performance.
We will offer you support and training to help you perform your job at your best, and we will give you the opportunity to achieve new professional goals.
If you are looking for a stimulating, flexible and rewarding job, don't hesitate to apply to join our team!

Requirements

Knowing another language besides your native language

To collaborate with us, it is necessary to possess fluent knowledge of at least one foreign language, in addition to your native language. In particular, you must be able to listen to and understand a dialogue in a foreign language.
If you are passionate about languages and have always wanted to challenge yourself as a translator and dubber, this could be the right opportunity for you.

Willingness to dub

If you want to participate in our project, the main requirement is to have the willingness to dub. You don't have to be a professional in the field, but you must be able to speak your native language correctly and without pronounced pronunciation defects or strong dialects.
Don't worry if you've never dubbed before, we'll teach you the basics to create products of sufficient quality. The important thing is to have a passion for this world and the determination to get involved.

A good microphone

If you want to be part of our project, it is important to have a high-quality microphone to ensure an optimal listening experience for our users.
If you don't have one, don't worry, we can recommend a mid-range model with an excellent quality-price ratio.
Don't miss this opportunity, join us and start creating high-quality content!

Zero earnings at the beginning

To access monetization and generate profits, a YouTube channel must meet certain metrics, which will be satisfied after several months of production, depending on how quickly the channel will grow.

This means that at the beginning, the earnings for you and for us will be zero. After a few weeks, however, when the channel starts to grow, you will receive the first modest monthly compensation.

You earn half of the profits
